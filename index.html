About Dr. John Crawfurd

Dr. John Crawfurd played a crucial role in Singapore’s history. He oversaw the implementation of the Jackson Plan, which helped develop the city, and he secured Singapore’s status through the 1824 Treaty of Friendship and Alliance. His policies led to increased population, trade, and revenue, shaping modern Singapore.

About the Crawfurd Scholarships

The Crawfurd Scholarships aim to support aspiring medical students at NUH Medical School. With the rising cost of education, many students face financial challenges. This scholarship helps students with strong academic records and financial need pursue their medical careers.

How to Apply

Follow these steps to apply:

  1. Complete the online or physical application form.
  2. Submit required documents (academic records, financial proof, and personal statement).
  3. Attend an interview if shortlisted.
  4. Wait for the final selection results.
